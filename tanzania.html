Livelihoods Project
This summer I will be volunteering in Tanzania for 10 weeks with Raleigh International on an International Citizen Service placement. My project involves encouraging sustainable livelihoods and enterprise development by working with groups and individuals in rural communities. I will be building community resilience through supporting young people and helping small businesses find ways to enhance the long term sustainability of their enterprises.
