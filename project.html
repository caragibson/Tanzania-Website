International Citizen Service and Raleigh International
Internationl Citizen Serivce (ICS) works with communities that have specifically requested their help. It aims to inspire young people in the UK and overseas to become active citizens who are passionate about long term community development. Raleigh International is a sustainable development charity that works for and with young people, alongside rural communities, to create positive and lasting change. Their work focuses on three areas: providing access to safe water and sanitation, protecting vulnerable environments and building livelihoods.
