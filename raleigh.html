International Citizen Service

International Citizen Service (ICS) works with communities that have specifically requested their help. It aims to inspire young people in the UK and overseas to become active citizens who are passionate about long term community development.

Find Out More About ICS

Raleigh International

Raleigh International is a sustainable development charity that works for and with young people, alongside rural communities, to create positive and lasting change. Their work focuses on three areas: providing access to safe water and sanitation, protecting vulnerable environments and building livelihoods.

Fundraising Challenge

My fundraising challenge was to raise £800 towards the work of Raleigh International and their ICS partners. The money contributed towards enabling the overall costs of ICS, so they can continue sending youth volunteers across the world to create lasting change and transform lives in some of the poorest communities. Overall, I raised £1496; I would like to thank every person who donated and supported me.

London 10km Run

On Monday 29th May, I completed the 2017 Vitality London 10,000 run. After months of training and running along the beach in St Andrews, I managed to run it in 59 minutes and 1 second. The race finished on the Mall approaching Buckingham Palace. Taking part in the race was an unforgettable experience.

Action at Home

After completing my project in Tanzania and returning home, I plan to continue making a difference in the UK community. I have created this website from scratch to share my story and inspire others to take action no matter where they are in the world.
